## 2026-07-08

Split this folder out of docs/product/role-gap-analysis.md. The trigger: realizing
that document conflated two different kinds of gaps under one label ("what's missing")
- some are things the repo should build (now docs/product/spec-queue.md), others are
things a person has to actually learn or experience, which no amount of additional
demo code substitutes for (futures mechanics, real customer discovery, Cowork,
working with a real engineering team under real constraints). Naming that difference
explicitly is itself the first entry: a repo can prove capability with a tool, but it
cannot prove domain judgment that only comes from real market participation or a real
team.
